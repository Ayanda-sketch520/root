About Us

Thusi's Bakery is a small local business located in Noordwyk, Midrand. The bakery provides fresh and affordable baked goods to the community.

We specialize in bread, cakes, muffins, and vetkoek (magwinya), all made fresh daily using quality ingredients.

We also offer online ordering and delivery services in Johannesburg and Midrand, making it easier for customers to enjoy our products from home.

Our goal is to provide excellent service, quality products, and grow into a trusted bakery brand in the community.
